Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked and drained
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 cup fried onions (birista)
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices: 2 bay leaves, 4 green cardamom pods, 4 cloves, 2-inch cinnamon stick Biryani masala powder (or a mix of ground cumin, coriander, and garam masala) Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, biryani masala, and salt for 30 minutes.
  2. Saut√© whole spices in oil in a large pot until fragrant.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and marinated chicken; cook until partially done.
  4. Layer soaked rice, fried onions, coriander, and mint.
  5. Pour water, bring to a boil, then simmer covered until rice is cooked and chicken is tender.
  6. Gently mix, garnish with more fried onions and herbs.
  7. Serve hot with raita. Enjoy your homemade chicken biryani!
